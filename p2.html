a denouement // a sea whistle 


a denouement

a sea whistle

balking at yet another rite of flattery

without another

stoned away

back against the wall

(the trill of a bird song)

the song bird

unclaimed and un-acclaiméd

never got-gotten-gutted there in the first place

arriving at 

appended to be

plant a cloud on the shore as a mast on a stern rampart of the afternoon sun well tarnished by now knowing the haze of declension 

a warm moth mouth moth again on a bare chest nestled against the fog 

believing to interrupt when necessary but not doing so any longer than

it all yields mortalité and a warm latte imprint and a weight of a feather in the morning 

the burning sun mocking geometry

lighter lingers and goodbye eyes

tantalizing
