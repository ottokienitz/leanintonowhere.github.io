a crack in the sidewalk 


a second cup of coffee


a crack in the sidewalk or


an avalanche in the sky


burned into the pavement below


ducking branches, intrepid blossoms, the roaming bee


honey, dancing sunlit shadows, melting


impress a tacit footprint in the sand


a mostly solid afternoon


slipping sometimes more often into dust


only illuminated in the glowing


passerby, reminding us of we


not all alone, but silhouetted


in the crack on the sidewalk


moving up and up and tumbling down


like curls


or the cadence of the sea


not from afar but within


a natural rhythm


only interrupted by


a crack in the sidewalk


or one or three


making us stop and


  
riting
